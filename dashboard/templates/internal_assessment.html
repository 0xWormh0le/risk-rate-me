Internal Assessments

Cybersecurity best practices for ensuring information asset confidentiality, integrity and availability

Protecting any business should follow a logical, deliberate method. An assessment of the organisation’s current security posture is the beginning of this process. After you have established your Public Risk Rating an internal security assessment should be conducted.

These self report assesments verify that basic cyber security controls are in place and functioning properly. These controls are low cost and effective, they help to protect your organisation against the most common cyber threats. 

Each assessment is based on existing cyber security frameworks and has been tailored to meet the needs of small companies so they can identify, assess and manage cybersecurity risks.

Once you have assessed your internal security you are in a better position to identify weaknesses and remediate them accordingly. NOTE: These assessments should be considered a guide only. They do not indicate compliance to the respective frameworks. More details on eah are provided on the respective pages.
